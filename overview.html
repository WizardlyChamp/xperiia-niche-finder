01  Overview

What CoreCue is

A self contained web tool that answers the question that stalls most creators before they start: which niche is actually right for me.

Instead of a personality gimmick, it reads real signals. What someone is drawn to, what they are good at, how they want to create, and how much they want to earn. It then scores every niche in its library against those signals and returns the three that fit best.

Each result is more than a label. It comes with why that niche pays, the formats that tend to work, the platforms to post on, and three specific posts to start with. Just before the result, the tool shows an honest earning potential chart across niches, with a clear note that audience size and consistency move income more than the niche label itself. That honesty is deliberate. It builds trust at the exact moment a creator is deciding who to listen to.

The tool sits on a real backend. Every completed quiz is saved to a private database and surfaced in a password protected dashboard, so the team behind it can see who took it, what they want, and where they are stuck.
